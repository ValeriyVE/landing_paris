Apartments

The aim was not to make the interior spaces look pompous or bulky, but breathe new life into renovated property by adding a lot of natural light and giving it a modern albeit contemporary look.

Interiors are a smart combination of practical and simple forms and materials. Natural wood, velvet, brass and art-deco elements appeal to any potential homeowners as they elegantly convey a bohemian contemporary Parisian style.

Layouts of apartments are designed to create entirely autonomous areas, even though walls in certain areas are absolutely absent, and therefore enhance the overall functionality of each individual space. In addition, large windows in every room provide plenty of natural sources of light and fill in the interiors with sunlight and life.
